Our story

Flowers, made simple & joyful.

Bloom Co. started with a simple idea: sending beautiful flowers should be as easy as it is delightful. No wilted servo bunches, no confusing florist jargon — just gorgeous, fresh arrangements that arrive when you need them.

We work hand-in-hand with local growers, arrange everything fresh each morning, and wrap it all in nothing but recycled paper and care.
