Our story

Fire is the whole point

Charcoal & Crust started with one belief: everything tastes better cooked over real fire. Our kebabs are marinated overnight and grilled on charcoal to order, and every pizza is stretched by hand and baked on stone at 400°C until the edges blister.

No heat lamps, no shortcuts — just Charcoal, Crust and a kitchen that fires up the moment you order.
