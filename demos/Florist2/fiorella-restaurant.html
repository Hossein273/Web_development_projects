Our story

From our famiglia to your table

Fiorella began with a wood-fired oven, a stack of family recipes, and a simple belief — that good food brings people together. Every dish is made by hand, the way it has been for generations.

We source our produce locally, pour wines we genuinely love, and treat every guest like they're sitting at our own kitchen table. Come hungry, leave happy.

— Giulia & Marco, owners
