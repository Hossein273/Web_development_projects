Our story

A little shop with
a lot of love.

Petal & Stem began with a market stall and a simple belief: that flowers should be fresh, seasonal, and grown close to home. Today we're a small Brisbane studio, still arranging every bouquet by hand.

We work with local growers to bring you blooms at their absolute best — and we wrap them in nothing but recycled paper and care.

— Mae & the Petal & Stem team
