Questions

Frequently asked

Do you really buy damaged or non-running cars?+

Absolutely. Running or not, damaged, high-kilometre, or even written off — we buy cars in any condition. Just be honest about the condition in your quote and we'll give you a fair offer.

How fast do I get paid?+

On the spot. The moment our driver inspects the car and signs the paperwork at pickup, you're paid by cash or instant bank transfer — before we tow it away.

Do I need a roadworthy or current rego to sell?+

No. You don't need a roadworthy certificate, and your car doesn't need to be registered. We handle unregistered and de-registered vehicles every day.

Is pickup really free?+

Yes — free pickup and towing anywhere across Brisbane and the Gold Coast, every time. There are no collection or towing fees deducted from your offer.

How is my offer calculated?+

Your offer is based on the make, model, year, condition, kilometres and whether it's registered. The instant figure is an estimate; we confirm the final price when we see the car in person.

What paperwork do I need?+

Just photo ID and proof you own the car (rego papers are ideal). We take care of the transfer and rego cancellation forms for you at pickup.
